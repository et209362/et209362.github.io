How to embed a YouTube video into your website

How to find the embed option on YouTube

  1. Go to YouTube and search for the video you need in the search bar. Once you've found the video open it and click the "share" button.

  2. Within the share button there is an option titled "embed". Click on it.

  3. Click the "copy" button in the bottom right of the window.

  4. Once you've copied the code from YouTube paste it into your text editor.

  5. You now have a YouTube embed <iframe> within your website.

How to edit the <iframe> HTML tag

  1. The <iframe> tag is what allows you to embed a video from an external source into your website.

  2. Within the <iframe> tag you will see the attributes src="", height="", width="" and title="". The src="" attribute is the link that will be used to add the YouTube embed. height="" and width="" will allow you to change the dimensions of the embed. Lastly, the title="" will be the title of the video.
